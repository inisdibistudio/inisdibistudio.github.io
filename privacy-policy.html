Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Inisdibi Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Solana Faucet mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the App, including claim history, referral activity, and wallet addresses you provide for withdrawals.
  • Device Information: We may collect device identifiers for push notification delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process coin claims and withdrawal requests
  • Manage your account and referral program
  • Send push notifications about claim availability
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We use Firebase Authentication for account management, Cloud Firestore for data storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications. Data is stored on servers located in regions managed by Google Cloud Platform.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Firebase/Google: For authentication, data storage, and notification services as described above.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at inisdibistudio@proton.me.
